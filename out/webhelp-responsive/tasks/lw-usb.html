How to Transfer a Document to the Laptop with USB

These instructions will show you how to tranfer documents onto the laptop directly from a USB

Before you begin:

  • Laptop

  • Laptop Power Cord

  • USB Drive (with document already uploaded)

  • Electrical Socket (in wall)

Steps

  1. Plug in the laptop power cord to the laptop's external power socket

  2. Plug in the plug side of the laptop power cord to the electrical socket

  3. Push the power button on the laptop

  4. Wait for the laptop to start up

  5. Plug in the USB drive in the laptop USB port


  6. Click on "My Computer" on your laptop

  7. Click on "(D:)" or "External Drive" to access files on your USB


  8. Click the document you want in the file and drag it onto the desktop

  9. Click the document now on your desktop

  10. Use the document now located on your desktop

  11. Unplug the USB drive as the document is now on your computer

    While most USBs nowdays are safe to remove without asking on the computer, check if your USB is a newer model

    If it is not, right click the USB symbol on the right side of the computer and click "Eject USB Mass Storage Device"
